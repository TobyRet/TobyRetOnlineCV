MY WORK

Product Management

In 2011 I was seconded to lead a cross departmental team on the creation of a new online community for music education professionals.

Built on the Drupal platform, the Youth Music Network has more than 6,000 members. 80% of the content is created by the community allowing the sharing of a wealth of expertise and know-how between professionals. Ongoing development is 'agile' and done in close consultation with users.

My success with the Youth Music Network lead to me being invited to speak about 'building online communities' at the Charity Comms Digital Impact Conference in 2013.

Branding

In 2013 I took the lead on Youth Music's first re-branding exercise. Youth Music had been through a lot of organisational change and needed to do more to engage supporters.

Working with Audience Communications I helped create a revised vision, mission and values for the Charity before creating a new visual identity with the help of M&C Saatchi.

Outputs included brand guidelines, new supporter website, re-skinned social media, showreel video and staff resources. The launch of the brand was met with great acclaim at a sold out Breakfast Seminar streamed live on the web.
