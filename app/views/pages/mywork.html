MY WORK

Online Communities

In 2011 I was seconded to lead a cross departmental team on the creation of a new online community for music education professionals.

Built on the drupal platform, the Youth Music Network today has more than 5,000 subscribers. 80% of the content is created by the community allowing the sharing of a wealth of expertise and know-how between professionals.

My success with the Youth Music Network lead to me being invited to speak about 'building online communties' at the Charity Comms Digital Impact Conference in 2013.


"Toby has been an excellent Project Manager delivering the new Youth Music Network on time. He has shown great tact and diplomacy in managing the team of people tasked with delivering the project. He is a great team player and has been a pleasure to work with." Andrew Jermey, Region & Nations Manager, Youth Music.

Branding

In 2013 I took the lead on Youth Music's first rebranding exercise. Youth Music had been through a lot of organisational change and needed to do more to engage supporters.

Working with Audience Communications I helped create a revised vision, mission and values for the Charity before creating a new visual identity with the help of M&C Saatchi.

Outputs included brand guidelines, reskinned supporter website and social media, showreel video and staff resources. The launch of the brand was met with great acclaim at a sold out Breakfast Seminar streamed live on the web.


"Love @youthmusic new circular branding - modern, focused & grown up in an intelligent way. Exudes trust too." Plymouth Music Zone.
